About

Whether you're a vinyl enthusiast or just a regular music lover, Groove Lounge is the place for you. Established in 2009, you will find a wide variety of records from jazz, soul, rock and many more. Sit back and enjoy a coffee while listening to your favourite artists. Live events are hosted weekly.

What to find in store:

  • Wide variety of vinyl records from different genres
  • Chill-out lounge area with comfortable seating
  • Weekly live events including open-mic nights and listening sessions
  • Knowledgeable staff to help you find your next favourite record
